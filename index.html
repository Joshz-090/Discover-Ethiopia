About Ethiopia

Land of Origins

Ethiopia is a country with a rich cultural heritage dating back thousands of years. It's home to ancient civilizations, remarkable archaeological finds, and stunning natural wonders.

From the rock-hewn churches of Lalibela to the Simien Mountains, Ethiopia offers a unique travel experience unlike any other in Africa.

The country is also the origin of coffee, with traditional coffee ceremonies being an integral part of Ethiopian culture.

Cultural Highlights

Rock-Hewn Churches of Lalibela

11 medieval monolithic churches carved out of rock

Simien Mountains National Park

Dramatic landscapes and unique wildlife including the Gelada baboon

Ancient Obelisks of Axum

Giant stelae marking the tombs of ancient rulers

Danakil Depression

One of the hottest and lowest places on Earth with surreal landscapes

Coffee Ceremony

A traditional ritual celebrating Ethiopia's gift of coffee to the world

Omo Valley Tribes

Home to diverse ethnic groups with unique traditions
